Two prototype greenhouses, two climates

The PhD runs between USMB (Savoie, FR) and the University of Genova (Imperia, IT).

🏔 Savoie, France

USMB site, at the foot of the Alps: an alpine, continental climate. Cooler, with a strong seasonal contrast and a heating-dominated winter; moderate irradiation.

🏖 Imperia, Italy

University of Genova site, on the Ligurian coast: a Mediterranean, maritime climate. Warmer and sunnier, with high summer irradiation and strong water-use pressure.
